Normal Skin

One would consider their skin type to be normal if they are able to describe it as neither oily nor dry. If there is ever a presence of oiliness or dryness, it is often rare and easy to resolve. Pores tend to be not easily visible and skin exhibits few lines and wrinkles. A normal skin type has a smooth and even skin tone with no marks or blemishes.
